About Me

Curious by nature,
driven by impact.

I'm Okikijesu Ezekiel Ìyànúolùwà — a product-minded builder with a deep passion for creating digital experiences that solve meaningful problems. I sit at the intersection of strategy, design thinking, and technology to build products people actually love.

My approach starts with understanding people — their pains, their goals, and the invisible friction in their everyday lives. From there, I translate insights into clear product visions, define MVPs with ruthless focus, and think in systems to build solutions that scale.

I'm particularly drawn to products that expand access and opportunity — tools for learners, creators, families, and communities that are often underserved by mainstream technology.

My Product Philosophy

01
Start with the problem, not the solution

Great products are born from deep understanding of real pain, not feature lists.

02
Build small, learn fast

An MVP shipped today beats a perfect product that never launches.

03
Empathy is a superpower

Understanding your users at a human level is the ultimate competitive advantage.

04
Clarity over complexity

The best products feel simple. That simplicity is the result of hard decisions.

How I Work

My product thinking
process.

🔍
Step 01

Discover

Observe real behavior, listen for pain, and ask better questions to uncover problems worth solving.

🎯
Step 02

Define

Frame the core problem clearly. Align on who the user is and what success truly looks like.

💡
Step 03

Ideate

Generate diverse solutions without constraints, then filter ruthlessly for impact and feasibility.

⚡
Step 04

Prioritize

Identify the smallest set of features that deliver the highest value — that's the MVP.

🚀
Step 05

Launch & Learn

Ship early, gather feedback, measure outcomes, and iterate toward product-market fit.
